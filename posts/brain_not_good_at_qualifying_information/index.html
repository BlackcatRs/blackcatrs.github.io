Brain Not Good at Qualifying Information

2023/12/14

🧠💡 Unlocking the Secrets of the Brain! 🚀

Ever wondered why our brains sometimes prioritize recently learned info over what's truly important? 🤔 It's like having a mental "Last In, First Out" (LIFO) system! 🔄

The brain tends to overestimate the importance of facts that are easily retrieved, creating a "Feature-positive" effect. 🌟 This means we might neglect valuable, harder-to-recall information in favor of the latest tidbits.

⚖️ But fear not! To balance this cognitive quirk, we need the support of external systems like notebooks or databases. 📚💻 Let's embrace the power of a synergistic brain-external system duo for more accurate thinking!
